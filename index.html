Cookie Care | Information

Soft cookies are best consumed in 3 days.

Store in zip lock or in an airtight container if will not be consumed immediately.

Some cookie will begin to harden and dry out if stored in refrigerator.

Cookies may retain its quality if stored in freezer. You may put a sliced of white bread inside the air tight container where you put the cookies. This will help the cookies stay soft as fresh bread will moist, keeping the cookie from drying out. HUMIDITY!

When removed on freezer, allow cookie to reach room temperature for 2 to 3 hours before consuming. The use of microwave for defrosting is discouraged.
